Slovenian president undergoes operation of prostate tumour
LJUBLJANA, Slovenia - Slovenia's President Danilo Tuerk has undergone an operation to remove a prostate tumour. The president's office said Thursday that Tuerk is recovering well after the surgery, which was carried out Tuesday in neighbouring Austria. The statement says that the tumour was detected at an early stage and successfully removed. It says that the president will return to work next week. The statement added that Tuerk paid for the operation himself.
