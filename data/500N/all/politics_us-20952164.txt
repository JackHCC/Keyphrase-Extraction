Reisch takes over as commander of SD National Guard
RAPID CITY, S.D. - The South Dakota National Guard made some leadership changes on Saturday. Brig. Gen. Timothy Reisch (rysch) was installed as state adjutant general in charge of the Army National Guard and Air National Guard. He replaces the retiring Maj. Gen. Steven Doohen in the top spot. Col. Jeffrey Marlette replaces Reisch as assistant state adjutant general. Both change-of-command ceremonies were in Rapid City.
