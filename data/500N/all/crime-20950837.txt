Tenn. police officer is shot and killed responding to robbery call; suspect in hospital
CHATTANOOGA, Tenn. - Authorities in Tennessee say a police officer was shot and killed while investigating a robbery. Chattanooga police spokeswoman Jerri Weary says the officer was responding to a call around 10:30 a.m. Saturday about a robbery at U.S. Money Shops. When officers arrived, they encountered a man who started shooting at police. A gun battle and foot pursuit ensued. An officer was shot and killed. The suspect was shot at least once and is being treated at a hospital. Neither the officer nor the suspect was identified.
