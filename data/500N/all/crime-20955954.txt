Man charged with attempted murder of Chloe West
A man has been charged with the attempted murder of a 14-year-old girl who was stabbed outside her school. Chloe West suffered serious wounds to her upper body and face near the gates of Ridgewood High School in Wollaston in the West Midlands on Friday. She was released from hospital earlier on Saturday. Samuel Tomlinson, 18, from Sedgley, West Midlands, is due to appear before Dudley magistrates on Monday. He is also accused of wounding another pupil. Witnesses said a man drove up to Chloe at about 0840 BST and leapt out of the car before pushing her to the floor and stabbing her. She was treated at Birmingham Children's Hospital.
