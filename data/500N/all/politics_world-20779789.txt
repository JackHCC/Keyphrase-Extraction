Tibet through the Lenses-Potala Palace
Special Report: This is Tibet This year marks the 60th anniversary of the peaceful liberation of Tibet. To celebrate the achievements the autonomous region has made, a series of photographs depicting the region's rich natural, cultural and religious resources will be released. The first part of the series is fully packed with pictures of Tibet's landmark structure - Potala Palace.
