NYPD Comm. Kelly: A beat cop from way back
NYPD commissioner Ray Kelly and City Council Public Safety Committee Chairman Peter Vallone Jr. - who generally agree on law enforcement issues - where on the same page musically Thursday night for Vallone's 50th birthday party at Central Lounge in Astoria.
