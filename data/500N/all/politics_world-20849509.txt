Gloucestershire man arrested for &#039;naked gardening&#039;
A Gloucestershire man has been charged with public order offences after he was arrested for allegedly committing lewd acts while gardening naked. Police said the 62-year-old was arrested after neighbours reported him gardening nude at his Cirencester home. A police spokesman said the indecency charge was not pursued but he had been charged with three public order offences. He has been summonsed to appear before Cheltenham magistrates on 6 May.
